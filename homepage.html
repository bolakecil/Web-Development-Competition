EASTERN GEM OF INDONESIA

Island gems, where azure waters embrace pristine shores, dragon dreams that unfold in the ancient landscapes, and a vibrant culture that weaves tales of Nusa Tenggara Timur (NTT) beckon adventurous souls. Dive into the mesmerizing reefs, trek through terraced rice paddies that whisper stories of centuries past, and stand witness to the majestic Komodo dragons, guardians of this extraordinary realm. Immerse yourself in the tapestry of ancient traditions, where every dance and ritual tells a story.

DIVE INTO OUR DIVERSITY

Thousand years of cultural heritage, preserved through the stories and words of our ancestors. Our people are known for progressing civilization while being one with nature. a mosaic of ethnic groups, languages, and traditions, making it a vibrant tapestry of Indonesian culture.

Traditional villages with their unique architecture, such as the cone-shaped houses of the Ngada people in Flores, offer a glimpse into the province's rich cultural heritage. Festivals and ceremonies, often accompanied by the hypnotic rhythms of traditional music and dance, are a testament to the community's strong connection to their ancestors and the land.
